Add field for new email used for sending follow-up emails

For use when follow-up emails should be sent to an email address different from standard email

This module provides anew email field - 'Statement of Account Email - that is set when follow-up emails should be sent to an email address that is different from the standard partner email.

Main Features:

  • New field 'Statement of Account Email' is added.
  • When filled the follow-up email is sent to this address.
  • If not filled the follow-up email will be sent normally to standard partner email.

When 'Statement of Account Email' is filled the follow-up email is sent to this email address:

If the new field is not filled then the follow-up email is sent to the standard partner email address.
